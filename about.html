About TTC

Building Excellence Through Craftsmanship and Innovation

At Takes Two Construction, we are more than just builders – we are craftsmen, innovators, and partners in bringing your vision to life. Founded on the principles of quality, integrity, and exceptional service, we have established ourselves as a trusted leader in the construction and renovation industry throughout Texas.
