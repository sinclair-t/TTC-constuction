Our Heritage

Built on a Foundation of Excellence

The story of Takes Two Construction is one of dedication, growth, and unwavering commitment to quality. From our humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in Texas construction, our heritage reflects the values and vision that continue to drive us forward.

Our Journey

Milestones in Our Story

2010

The Beginning

Takes Two Construction was founded with a simple vision: to provide exceptional construction services built on integrity, quality, and client satisfaction. Starting with small residential projects, we quickly established a reputation for reliability and craftsmanship.

2013

Expanding Services

As our reputation grew, we expanded our services to include custom home renovations and additions. This marked the beginning of our specialization in transforming existing spaces into dream homes for our clients.

2016

Outdoor Living Expertise

Recognizing the growing demand for outdoor living spaces, we developed expertise in custom pools, spas, and outdoor entertainment areas. This expansion allowed us to offer comprehensive solutions for both indoor and outdoor living.

2019

Technology Integration

We embraced cutting-edge construction technologies and project management systems, enhancing our ability to deliver projects on time and within budget while maintaining our high standards of quality.

2022

Sustainable Practices

Committed to environmental responsibility, we integrated sustainable construction practices and green building techniques into our services, helping clients create eco-friendly spaces that reduce environmental impact.

2024

Continued Growth

Today, Takes Two Construction stands as a trusted partner for residential and commercial construction projects throughout Texas, continuing to build on our heritage of excellence while looking toward an innovative future.

Our Legacy

Building for Generations

Our heritage is not just about the years we've been in business – it's about the lasting relationships we've built, the communities we've served, and the standards we've set. Every project we complete becomes part of our legacy, a testament to our commitment to excellence.

We take pride in the fact that many of our clients return to us for additional projects and refer us to their friends and family. This trust, built over years of consistent performance, is the true measure of our heritage.
